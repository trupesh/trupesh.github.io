About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at Karnavati University with 11 years of professional experience in academia and research. Currently, I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Nirma University, focusing on advanced topics in distributed and federated learning systems.

My research interests encompass Federated Learning, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Delay Tolerant Networking, and Computer Vision. I am particularly passionate about developing privacy-preserving and secure machine learning solutions for healthcare and IoT applications.

I have completed an international internship in Data Science and Medical Imaging at Advanced Research LLC, Florida, USA, and actively contribute to the academic and research community through publications, workshops, and mentoring.
